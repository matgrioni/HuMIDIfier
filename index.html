HuMIDIfier

Make wet beats.
Convey your musical thoughts into sound.

W-w-w-whats going on!?

HuMIDIfier allows you to sing into the mic on your computer and hear the notes you hum output by a guitar, piano, drum etc.

Accurately portray the ideas in your mind into the instrument of your choice realtime! HuMIDIfier is also capable of creating melody backings for different genres.
